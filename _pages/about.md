Hi (Xin chào 🇻🇳)! I'm Minh, a first-year PhD student at the National University of Singapore (NUS), advised by [Prof. Bryan Kian Hsiang Low](https://scholar.google.com.tw/citations?user=2P-Q09UAAAAJ). I then worked as an AI resident at [FPT Software AI Center](https://www.linkedin.com/company/fpt-software-ai-center/) under the supervision [Dr. Nghi D. Q. Bui](https://bdqnghi.github.io/).
